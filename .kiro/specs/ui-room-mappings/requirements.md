# Requirements Document

## Introduction

The Room Mappings page (/room-mappings) manages the association between Amello room names and Booking.com room names. It supports manual mapping, AI auto-mapping with confidence scoring, and bulk acceptance workflows.

## Glossary

- **Room_Mappings_Page**: The page (/room-mappings) managing Amello-to-Booking room name mappings with AI auto-mapping support
- **AI_Mapping**: A mapping suggestion generated by Claude AI with a confidence score, stored with source='ai' until manually accepted

## Requirements

### Requirement 1: Room Mappings Management

**User Story:** As a user, I want to map Amello room names to Booking.com room names, so that the rate comparison can match rooms accurately.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Room_Mappings_Page SHALL display a global header with the total unmapped count badge and a back link to Rate Comparison
2. THE Room_Mappings_Page SHALL provide an AI Auto-Mapping card with a "Run AI for All Hotels" button that processes hotels sequentially, displays a progress bar, and saves all AI suggestions to the database with source='ai'
3. THE Room_Mappings_Page SHALL provide a filter bar with hotel search input and filter buttons (All/Mapped/Unmapped/Pending Approval)
4. THE Room_Mappings_Page SHALL render per-hotel collapsible sections with badges indicating: unmapped count, AI suggestions count, AI pending count, or fully mapped status
5. WHEN a mapping has source "manual", THE Room_Mappings_Page SHALL display the booking room name with a "manual" badge and edit/delete buttons
6. WHEN a mapping has source "ai", THE Room_Mappings_Page SHALL display the booking room name with an "AI" badge, confidence percentage, and Accept/Change/Delete buttons
7. WHEN an amello room is unmapped but has an AI suggestion available, THE Room_Mappings_Page SHALL display the suggestion in italic with an "AI suggestion" badge, confidence percentage, reasoning text, and Confirm/Change buttons
8. WHEN an amello room is unmapped without any suggestion, THE Room_Mappings_Page SHALL display a "+ Map room" button that opens a dropdown with available booking rooms
9. THE Room_Mappings_Page SHALL provide an "Accept All" button per hotel to bulk-accept all pending AI mappings
